News & Media

A landmark partnership between Paninian Technologies and DRDO marks a significant step in India's push toward indigenous autonomous defence systems. The collaboration focuses on developing next-generation combat aerial vehicles for the Indian armed forces.

The Svayatt M1 combat aerial vehicle successfully completed its first fully autonomous flight trial, demonstrating onboard navigation, obstacle avoidance, and mission execution without human intervention.

Paninian Technologies has been selected for the NVIDIA Inception Programme, gaining access to cutting-edge GPU computing resources and AI development tools to accelerate onboard intelligence.

Godrej Aerospace and Paninian Technologies have signed a Memorandum of Understanding to jointly develop indigenous propulsion systems for next-generation unmanned combat aerial vehicles.
